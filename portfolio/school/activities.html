Student Activities & Clubs

Beyond the classroom: Explore your passions and talents.

Engage, Explore, Excel

At Springfield High, we believe that a well-rounded education extends beyond academics. Our vibrant array of student activities, clubs, and athletic programs provides invaluable opportunities for personal growth, leadership development, teamwork, and community engagement. There's something for everyone!

Athletics

Springfield High boasts a proud tradition of athletic excellence. We offer a wide range of competitive sports for both boys and girls across all seasons, including:

  • Basketball, Soccer, Volleyball
  • Track & Field, Cross Country, Swimming
  • Football, Baseball, Softball, Tennis
  • And more!

Clubs & Organizations

Discover new interests and connect with peers through our diverse selection of student-led clubs. From academic honor societies to creative arts and service groups, possibilities include:

  • Debate Club, Robotics Club, Chess Club
  • Drama Club, Art Club, School Newspaper
  • Environmental Club, Community Service Club
  • Student Government, National Honor Society

Performing Arts

Unleash your creativity and talent on stage or behind the scenes. Our performing arts programs offer opportunities in:

  • School Musical & Drama Productions
  • Concert Band, Orchestra, Choir

  • Dance Team

Student Leadership

We empower students to take on leadership roles through various avenues, including Student Government Association, class officers, club presidents, and peer mentorship programs. These opportunities cultivate responsibility, decision-making skills, and the ability to inspire others.
